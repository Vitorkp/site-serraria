About us

Tradition, scale and industrial quality

Founded in 1995, PinusVale is located in São José do Norte, Rio Grande do Sul. The company operates in the industrial production of pine wood, focusing on products for the international market.

Our continuous production system allows transforming wood from tree arrival to the final product ready for drying, packaging and export.
